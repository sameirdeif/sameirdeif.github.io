About Me

Leading the verification and validation of advanced electromagnetic simulation tools such as Ansys HFSS, SIwave, and Q3D Extractor. Collaborating with cross-functional teams to ensure the accuracy and reliability of simulation results, and providing technical support for complex RF and microwave design projects.

Experienced Electromagnetics and Microwave Engineer with a Ph.D. in Electromagnetics and Microwave Engineering from the University of Alberta, I have dedicated over a decade to mastering the complexities of RF and microwave system design, signal integrity (SI), and power integrity (PI). My work is rooted in a deep understanding of electromagnetic theory, complemented by hands-on experience with industry-leading simulation tools such as Ansys HFSS, SIwave, and Q3D Extractor. My career has been driven by a passion for innovation, leading to significant contributions in the development and validation of cutting-edge technologies across various high-impact industries.

At Ansys Inc., I have played a pivotal role in the verification and validation of advanced electromagnetic simulation software, ensuring their accuracy and reliability in solving complex engineering challenges. My role involves collaborating closely with cross-functional teams, providing technical expertise, and leading efforts to optimize power delivery networks and mitigate signal integrity issues. This experience has not only honed my technical skills but has also reinforced my ability to communicate complex concepts effectively to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Beyond my technical acumen, I am committed to continuous learning and professional development. I am actively engaged in exploring the latest advancements in SI/PI methodologies and their applications in emerging technologies. My dedication to staying at the forefront of the industry enables me to deliver innovative solutions that push the boundaries of what’s possible. I am passionate about using my expertise to contribute to projects that have a meaningful impact, whether through enhancing the performance of consumer electronics or advancing the capabilities of communication systems.
